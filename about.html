🏸 About DK's Badminton Club

Founded in 2015, DK's Badminton Club has become a trusted name for badminton enthusiasts. We offer 4 premium wooden courts, professional lighting, and a supportive community.

Our mission is simple: promote fitness, sportsmanship, and the joy of badminton. Whether you're picking up a racket for the first time or training for competitions, we have a place for you.

👨‍🏫 Our Head Coach

Coach DK – Over 6+ years of coaching experience. He specializes in footwork, strategy, and stamina. Under his guidance, many students have won district-level championships.

Our assistant coaches are certified professionals who ensure every player gets personalized attention.
